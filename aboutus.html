Who we are

We are Ireland’s premier pre-purchase vehicle inspection service. The car and van inspection service we offer is both personal and friendly. You deal directly with the engineer.

Our car inspection engineers are members of the Institute of Automotive Engineer Assessors or the Institute of the Motor Industry or both. They have considerable experience in vehicle inspections , having completed a recognised apprenticeship and have many years post apprenticeship experience. They have obtained qualifications from the basic car trade examinations up to advanced levels in Motor Car Engineering. They have taught Motor Car Engineering to both apprentices and adults. They are also experienced at acting as expert witnesses in court hearings related to cars, vans and other motor vehicles.

We can conduct vehicle inspections anywhere within the greater Dublin area on the east coast of Ireland.
